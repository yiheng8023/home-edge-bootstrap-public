# Sponsoring

Sponsorship is voluntary. Any amount is appreciated and supports ongoing maintenance,
documentation, testing, and community operations.

Reviewed sponsorship channels:

- CNY: the WeChat Pay and Alipay QR codes in [README.md](README.md).
- Cross-border or other supported currencies:
  [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/ncp/payment/LNTF8KXGJXMZY). Available currencies, payment
  methods, conversion, and fees are determined by the PayPal checkout page and may vary by country
  or region.

Verify the displayed recipient before confirming a payment. Do not send funds through accounts,
links, or QR codes that are not published in this repository.

Sponsorship is not a charitable donation or tax deduction and does not purchase a support SLA,
security warranty, response priority, release timing, or feature. Sponsors do not receive release
authority, security exceptions, guaranteed response times, or control over technical decisions.
